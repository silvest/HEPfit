Detailed Description

A class for the binned differential observable \(<dBR>\) in \(M \to P l^+l^-\).

Author
HEPfit Collaboration
Copyright
GNU General Public License

This class is used to compute the binned differential observable \(<BR>\) in \(M \to P l^+l^-\) in terms of the binned CP averaged helicity coefficients \(<\Sigma_i>\), computed in the MPll class, and the meson width \(W_M\):

\[ <dBR>= \frac {<d BR>}{<d q^2>} \,. \]
